Outside4Life was created by Avid4 Adventure to encourage kids to be active outdoors. Log your kid's outdoor activity hours, and cheer them on as they climb up our scoreboard!


How to play:


- Create a family Profile and aliases for each of your kids


- Go to the Feed to post your kid's outdoor activity and hours spent outdoors each day. Check out the exciting activities other kids are doing in the Feed.


- Encourage your kids to get to the top of our Scoreboard!
